Customizable Text Shortener

Have you ever tried to type something somewhere but get told your message exceeds the character limit? This simple Javascript app can shorten your message with minor visual differences by replacing certain strings of letters with individual characters that look similar. Enter your text, choose what replacements you want to make, and click the button to shorten the text!
